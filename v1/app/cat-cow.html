Cat–cow stretch

What this helps: keeps your spine loose and eases stiffness through your back.

Steps

  1. 1Rest on your hands and knees, with your hands under your shoulders.
  2. 2Slowly arch your back and lift your head to look ahead.
  3. 3Then round your back and gently tuck your chin toward your chest.
  4. 4Move slowly between the two, in time with your breath.

Flow gently for 30 seconds

30 seconds
