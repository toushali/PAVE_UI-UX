Roll-up

What this helps: builds control through your spine and gently strengthens your stomach.

Steps

  1. 1Sit tall with your legs out in front and a soft bend in your knees.
  2. 2Reach your arms forward at shoulder height.
  3. 3Slowly curl down one bone at a time toward the floor.
  4. 4Pause, then roll back up just as slowly.

Move slowly for 30 seconds

30 seconds
