Box breathing

What this helps: calms your body and eases tension with slow, steady breaths.

Steps

  1. 1Sit comfortably and let your shoulders relax.
  2. 2Breathe in slowly through your nose for a count of four.
  3. 3Hold your breath gently for a count of four.
  4. 4Breathe out for four, pause for four, and repeat.

Breathe slowly for 60 seconds
