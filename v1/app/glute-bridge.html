Glute bridge

What this helps: strengthens the muscles that support your lower back and hips.

Steps

  1. 1Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  2. 2Gently tighten your stomach muscles.
  3. 3Slowly lift your hips until your body makes a straight line.
  4. 4Hold, then lower back down slowly.

Hold for 15 seconds

15 seconds
